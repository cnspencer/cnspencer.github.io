About Buddy:

He's a mutt (probably terrier and poodle) from Richland, WA and has lived there with his adopted family for 7 years. Despite his age, he is still very active and plays with whatever playmate he can find.

Buddy has done minimal acting due to contract troubles with Spencer Studios and Cousins Ltd.
