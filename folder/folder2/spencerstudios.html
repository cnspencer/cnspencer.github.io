About Spencer Studios

Spencer Studios is a low budget film company founded by a pair of sisters. The Studios frequently partners with Paul Productions to create movies such as their most infamous series Angelic Journey. Rumours are abounding about another partnership in yet another franchise but both companies have confirmed nothing. However, the Studios has announced that a portion of the music in whatever they do next will be composed specifically for it.

Despite their huge talent and fame on YouTube, they are a virtually invisible company.

To remain in budget, most music is from royalty-free artist Kevin MacLeod. Their fan Youtube channel is also run by founder CNSpencer.
