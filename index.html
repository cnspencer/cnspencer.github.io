Cooper the Cat

The official webpage of the infamous Cooper the Cat with officially released photos.



























About Cooper:

He is a white american short-hair cat with orange spots on his back and head. He is from Richland, WA and lives with his adopted family there. He was bought for $20 and given to his owner for her birthday. Cooper enjoys napping in the catnip and doesn't like belly rubs, cuddling, and just people in general. He has been known to be fussy about his roles during filming and often causes the only major mistakes in the movies that he stars in. Cooper is a little more than 16 months old (which is sorta like 20 years in cat years). He lives happily* with a grey tabby cat, Misty and likes to play with a dog named Buddy. He spends his day switching between bedrooms to sleep in, eating, lounging in the lawn, and bothering Misty whenever he can.

Cooper has starred mainly in Spencer Studios and Paul Productions films. He'd like to do more but nothing has been planned yet.

*somewhat or unhappily

Fun facts:

On Cooper's first trip to the vet, he briefly stopped breathing, but was quickly resesitated. When he came home, he was blind and therefore the most cuddly he has ever been. :3 His vision was restored a few days after.

Cooper has an unusually long body (almost like a wiener dog...but a cat) as well as a blatantly long tail.
