Musical Pieces

Title: Christmas Hymn

A medley of some classic Christmas hymns. Originally made for a band class assignment and played as a French Horn duet. (2014)


Title: Silent River (Minecraft Noteblock Version)

Title: Silent River (Synthesizer Version)

One of my first full-length songs, "Silent River" was a catchy melody played only on the black keys. It was originally about 12 seconds long and eventually (years later) heavily modified and revised to include all the current reprises. The Noteblock version was created in 2017. (2013-2015)


Title: Glorious Victory

Essentially my first composition, it carries the somber, bittersweet ambiance one could imagine in surveying a battle's aftermath. Meant to be a clarinet duet. (2012)


Title: Under the Water Main Menu Song

This song was made for a DVD menu and only took about an hour to compose from start to finish. (2016)


Title: Song I Found Here

While the title isn't very creative, it describes the time I found a song I had started but had no idea where or when it came to be. I quickly named the file with a plan to formally name it later (which never happened). As such, it is quite short since I never figured out what do with the start. (2016?)


Title: Seemingly Possible

Simple and perhaps a little repetitive, but had some decent transitions. (2014)


Title: Common Thoughts

Another of the earlier songs, it was heavily influenced by pieces we played in band class. (2014)


Title: Light Valley Scene

Precursor to the song "Anya's Noble Death" and made for the same movie. Unfortunately, the rest of the soundtrack was never finished for said movie. (2016)


Title: Anya's Noble Death

It has a rough start but was fine when paired with the scene it was written for. The ending is abrupt as the pickup notes were meant to transition to another scene, but was scrapped when we were pushed to the deadline. (2016)

Choice Pieces from Esmerelda's Wrath (2019)

Title: Esmerelda

As the crowning musical piece of Esmerelda's Wrath, this brief composition starts with the mysterious and building theme of the Princess. It turns downwardly at the appearance of Esmerelda and carries her tune strongly through the end. (2019)


Title: Noble Sacrifice

This piece starts with an uneasy feeling, as if something dark looms over. It picks up the pace when evil soldiers appear and block the way. The violins shout a bitter dissonance as a friend falls to save the others. The song moves into the disappointing training session as their real mentor is now gone. (2019)


Title: Trapped

"Trapped" conveys that message of hope that our hero desperately needed to get back into the right mindset. Though trapped, the Princess knows that they must stop the evil Esmerelda. Then, the guards come. (2019)


Title: Kitty

A desperate cry for help that ends with a happy and light-hearted resolution. (2019)
